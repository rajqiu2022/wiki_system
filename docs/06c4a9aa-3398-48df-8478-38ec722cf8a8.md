## 1.Introduction

This MaTouch 7â board is based on the ESPRESSIF New ESP32P4 controller, which is based on RISC-V, plus a ESP32 C6 module for WIFI/Bluetooth. With 7 inch 1024\*600 capacitive Touch/ 2 Million Pixels Camera / microphone / Speaker integrated, making this product a good candidate for multimedia development based on ESP32 solutions.

ESP32P4 packs two RISC-V cores running at up to 400MHz with 32MB of pseudo-static RAM (PSRAM) and 16MB of flash storage, providing not only an impressive amount of CPU performance but hardware acceleration for image encoding and decoding. The two-lane MIPI CSI and DSI ports on the board is target the device at human-machine interaction projects.
