# H-Bridge Motor Shield

## Introduction

This H-Bridge Motor Shield based on 8 N-channel MOSFET IRF3205S and 4 pcs half-bridge motor controller IR2104, to build 2 H-bridge. With this shield, you can control two DC motors with a max current up to 8A with 22v voltage at the same time. Also, you can control the motor direction by the logic 0 or 1 with the digital pin and speed by the PWM PIN of your MCU, like Arduino. And we support the Screw interface, it is easy to connect to the motor and power.

Model:[OAS0000HB](https://www.makerfabs.com/h-bridge-motor-shield.html)

![HBridge_Motor_Shield.jpg](https://www.makerfabs.com/image/wiki_image/2023-03-27_19_00_37_0.jpg)

## Features

- Operating Voltage: 6 - 22V
- Continuous output current: up to 8 Amps
- Peak output current: 15 Amps
- PWM Duty: 0~99%
