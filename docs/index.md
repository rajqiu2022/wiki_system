## About Makerfabs

Makerfabs is a hardware facilitation company based in Shenzhen, China. With the biggest electronic market, local manufacturing capabilities, and convenient global logistics system, we create many interesting and exciting products every day. Integrating resources to serve a new era of innovation.

### Products & Services

We provide a variety of:

**Products:**
- Various development platforms
- Modules and tools
- Electronic components
- Other open-source hardware

**Services:**
- Fusion PCB service
- Component procurement service
- PCB assembly service
